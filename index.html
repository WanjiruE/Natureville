Naivasha Road 8 minutes from Junction Mall

Natureville Valley Apartments

Natureville Valley Apartments  is a modern apartment block of 25 units of  2  bedrooms and 1 Bedrooms  located in Nairobi along Naivasha road. It is located 10 minutes from Junction Mall and close to the New Naivas Mall in an urban and secure neighborhood. Its 70 metres (3rd row) from the main Naivasha road. Great care and thought has been incorporated in the designing of each apartment ensuring modern design, spacious rooms, inviting ambiance and all-round practicality truly making it “Affordable Urban Living”. 

Features

– 1 & 2 Bedroom En-suite apartments
– A plinth area of approximately 80 square meters.
– Adequate Parking.
– Master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom.
– High quality PG Bison Joinery
– A modern American kitchen.
– A private balcony/dhobi area.
– Electric wall boundary fence with CCTV.
– Solar heating and backup generator in common areas.
– Quick access to Westlands through the 4 lane Waiyaki Way
